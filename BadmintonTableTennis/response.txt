I should talk to my doctor. They're the experts, and they can explain everything in detail. They can tell me about the different treatments available, like surgery, chemotherapy, or maybe radiation. I need to know what's going to work best for me.

I'm also going to need to think about my lifestyle. I know that staying healthy is crucial, so I should focus on eating well and exercising. These habits can help my body cope with the treatment and maybe even improve my chances.
